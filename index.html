What is Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock?

"Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock" is a variation of the popular game "Rock, Paper, Scissors". In this variation, the game has five gestures instead of three: Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, and Spock. Each gesture beats two other gestures and loses to the other two gestures. The rules for each gesture's victory or defeat are pre-determined, making it a game of strategy as well as chance.

Rules

The rules for Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock (RPSLS) are:

  • Rock crushes Scissors
  • Scissors cut Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors decapitate Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock
